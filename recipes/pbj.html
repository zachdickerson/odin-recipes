Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

The Father of all Sandwhiches

This Tasty Treat, is composed of the most delectable substance on Earth... Peanut Butter, and also we can NOT forget about the sweet tasty nectar called Jelly. These two powerful substances are smashed together inbetween two pieces of bread for ultimate flavor.

Ingredients

  • Jif Peanut Butter
  • Smuckers Grape Jelly
  • Wonder Bread

Steps

  1. Get a plate and knife from your kitcken.

  2. Take your Wonder Bread, and grab two slices. Toasting bread optional.

  3. Take your Jif Peanut Butter Jar and remove the lid, insert knife in peanut butter, remove knife, and swipe gently across one slice of bread.

  4. Take your Smucker Grape Jelly Jar and remove the lid, insert knife in jelly, remove knife, and swipe gently across one slice of bread.

  5. Place both piece of bread in your hands; peanut butter bread in right hand, jelly in the left hand. With one swift and percise movement smash the pieces together.

  6. THE MOST IMPORTANT STEP... Diagonally cut the bread (Pictued below).
  7. Enjoy :)
