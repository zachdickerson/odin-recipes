Hot Dog

Description

Know as the Grill-Out Special

This Glorious Sandwhich is as simple as it get... Meat and a Bun. Of course it doesn't hurt to squeze on some Mustard and Ketchup to get the party going.

Ingredients

  • Ballpark Hot Dog Buns
  • Vienna Beef Franks
  • Heinz Ketchp
  • Pohlmans Mustard

Optional Ingredients

  • Chile
  • Onions
  • Hot Peppers
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to 250 to 325 degrees, place Hot Dogs on grill, and cook for 5-7 minutes (Temp and cook times vary).

  2. Remove Hot Dog, and place it gently in your Ball Park Bun.

  3. Next add your the toppings, squirt your Pohlmans Mustard and Heinz Ketchup in alternating Squiggle lines.

  4. Enjoy :)
