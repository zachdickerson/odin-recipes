Curry Chicken

Description

Rich tasting, spicy delight known as Chicken Curry has a been a delicacy for thousands of years.

This recipe is creating from tweaking recipes from online to my liking, after trial and error.

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Chicken Breast
  • 2 Cups Plain Yourt
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 4 Tablespoons Curry Powder
  • 4 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 4 Teaspoon Paprika
  • 4 Tablespoon Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoon Garma Masla
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Fresh Grinded Ginger
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 1 Chopped Large Red Onion
  • 2 Chopped Red Bell Peppers
  • 4 Chopped Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Tablespoons Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil

Optional Ingredients

  • Parsley
  • Rice
  • Naan

Steps

  1. Our Prep Step, pull-out that knife we want to take the 2 LB's of Chicken Breast into 1 to 2 diameter inch sized cubes.

  2. Then, take our newly Cubbed Breasts, place in large plastic bag or bowl, mix in 2 Cups Plain Yourt, 2 Tablespoons Curry Powder, 2 Teaspoons Ground Cinnamon, 2 Teaspoon of Paprika, 2 Tablespoons Cayenne Pepper, 1 Tablespoons Garma Masla. Place bag or bowl in fridge for 30 minutes, or Best Results over night!

  3. Once ready to cook, set that grill to 400 degrees fahrenheit. This step is optional but I like to place chicken on kabobs and give them a good char on their outsides. Otherwise placing them on the grill for a couple minutes each side will do the trick.

  4. While or after grilling you can prep the final part of the chicken curry which is the sauce. Place Remaining Ingredients in a sauce pan on medium to high heat, cook until all mixted together.

  5. From here we add in the chicken into our sauce. Set temp to a simmer for 10-15 minutes.

  6. Your Chicken Curry is now complete. Add Parsley, and eat with Rice and Naan for extra substance and taste!
